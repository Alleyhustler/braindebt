Shipspotting

I wouldn't really consider myself to be a 'ship-spotter', but I do stop often to take pictures of ships. Whenever I'm in a new area I am curious about what kind of boats are in the area, and how many there are. I love to see different types of vessels and always have my eye out for 'rare' types of ships, like heavy-lift and buoy-tending boats.
