I became absolutely enthralled with Lamento after finally getting my hands on a physical copy of the game. Lamento feels so special to me; from the beautiful 2000s-2010s era aesthetics surrounding it, to the enchanting verdant setting of the game and the beautiful soundtrack to accompany the story. It invokes a sense of deep nostalgia for the 2010s in me. Konoe's journey to make sense of his strange circumstance and his desire to keep going despite the uncertainty of the world around him is very compelling to me. My favorite characters were Konoe and Leaks, and I liked Rai the most out of the bachelors.
