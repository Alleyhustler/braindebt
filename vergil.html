Notes on my affliction...
I was not born on Eorzea- in fact, the first half of my life is absent from my mind. No matter what length I go to I cannot jog my memories of the past. There is a hole in my head, and in my heart too, that I am always trying to fill up with my experiences; though I have my friends to thank for aiding in my efforts and following my journey to uncover my past- my apprentice, most of all. I don't often dream- sometimes I barely sleep, but when I do I dream vividly of a familiar place just out of my reaches, and I can't help but feel longingly as if I live the other half of my life there in my dreams. When I allow it to, the frustration of having my answers so close nearly drives me mad, but like to I think that it has tempered my spirit and made me a better man to learn to let go as I have.
Observations of myself...
I wish that I could relax more- but I'm the type to always keep my mind occupied and can't stand to sit still for long. My scholastic pursuits take up much of my spare time and, perhaps because of the old books I read, I have been told that I speak in an old-fasioned manner. To cut loose and to spend more time in nature I've taken up angling recently. My greatest natural talent is that I can always tell what the weather is going to be like the moment that I step outside- which comes in quite handily for my fishing pursuits. When I find people that I like I tend to find myself quickly caught up in their problems and sometimes their schemes, and often without even realising I've done so.
